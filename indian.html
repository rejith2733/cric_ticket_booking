INDIAN CRICKET ACHIEVEMENTS

World Cup Titles

India has won the Cricket World Cup twice in history: in 1983 and in 2011.

ICC Champions Trophy

The Indian cricket team has won the ICC Champions Trophy twice, in 2002 and 2013.

ICC World Twenty20

India clinched the ICC World Twenty20 title once in 2007.

Number 1 Test Ranking

India has held the number 1 position in the ICC Test Team Rankings for multiple years.

Asian Cup

India has been the champions of the Asia Cup multiple times.
