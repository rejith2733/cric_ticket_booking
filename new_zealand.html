NEW ZEALAND CRICKET ACHIEVEMENTS

Cricket World Cup Final Appearances

The New Zealand cricket team has reached the final of the Cricket World Cup twice, in 2015 and 2019.

ICC Champions Trophy Title

New Zealand won the ICC Champions Trophy once, in 2000.

ICC T20 World Cup Performances

New Zealand has performed well in the ICC T20 World Cup, reaching the semi-finals on multiple occasions.

Individual Achievements

New Zealand cricketers have achieved many individual milestones, including:

  • Most Test runs: Kane Williamson
  • Most Test wickets: Richard Hadlee
  • Highest individual Test score: Brendon McCullum (302 runs)

Dominance in Limited-Overs Cricket

New Zealand has shown strong performances in limited-overs cricket, with notable successes in ICC tournaments and bilateral series.
