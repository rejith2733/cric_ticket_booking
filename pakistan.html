PAKISTAN CRICKET ACHIEVEMENTS

Cricket World Cup Title

The Pakistan cricket team won the Cricket World Cup once, in 1992.

ICC Champions Trophy Title

Pakistan won the ICC Champions Trophy once, in 2017.

ICC T20 World Cup Title

Pakistan clinched the ICC T20 World Cup once, in 2009.

Individual Achievements

Pakistani cricketers have achieved many individual milestones, including:

  • Most Test runs: Younis Khan
  • Most Test wickets: Wasim Akram
  • Highest individual Test score: Hanif Mohammad (337 runs)

Dominance in Limited-Overs Cricket

Pakistan has shown strong performances in limited-overs cricket, with notable successes in ICC tournaments and bilateral series.
