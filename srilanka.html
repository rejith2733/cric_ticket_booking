SRI LANKA CRICKET ACHIEVEMENTS

Cricket World Cup Title

The Sri Lankan cricket team won the Cricket World Cup once, in 1996.

ICC Champions Trophy Title

Sri Lanka won the ICC Champions Trophy once, in 2002.

ICC T20 World Cup Title

Sri Lanka clinched the ICC T20 World Cup once, in 2014.

Individual Achievements

Sri Lankan cricketers have achieved many individual milestones, including:

  • Most Test runs: Kumar Sangakkara
  • Most Test wickets: Muttiah Muralitharan
  • Highest individual Test score: Sanath Jayasuriya (340 runs)

Dominance in Limited-Overs Cricket

Sri Lanka has shown strong performances in limited-overs cricket, with notable successes in ICC tournaments and bilateral series.
