ENGLAND CRICKET ACHIEVEMENTS

Cricket World Cup Titles

The England cricket team won their first Cricket World Cup title in 2019.

ICC Champions Trophy Titles

The England cricket team has won the ICC Champions Trophy once, in 2013.

Ashes Series Victories

England has had numerous successful Ashes series over the years, both at home and away.

Individual Achievements

English cricketers have achieved many individual milestones, including:

  • Most Test runs: Alastair Cook
  • Most Test wickets: James Anderson
  • Highest individual Test score: Len Hutton (364 runs)

Dominance in Limited-Overs Cricket

The England cricket team has shown strong performances in limited-overs cricket, including reaching the final of the ICC T20 World Cup in 2016.
