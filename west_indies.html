WEST INDIES CRICKET ACHIEVEMENTS

Cricket World Cup Titles

The West Indies cricket team has won the Cricket World Cup twice:

  • 1975
  • 1979

ICC Champions Trophy Titles

The West Indies cricket team has not won the ICC Champions Trophy yet.

T20 World Cup Titles

The West Indies cricket team has won the ICC T20 World Cup twice:

  • 2012
  • 2016

Individual Achievements

West Indian cricketers have achieved numerous individual milestones:

  • Most Test runs: Brian Lara
  • Most Test wickets: Courtney Walsh
  • Highest individual Test score: Brian Lara (400 not out)

Dominance in Limited-Overs Cricket

The West Indies cricket team has been known for its dominance in limited-overs cricket, with many notable performances in ICC tournaments and bilateral series.
