SOUTH AFRICA CRICKET ACHIEVEMENTS

Cricket World Cup Semi-Final Appearances

The South African cricket team has reached the semi-finals of the Cricket World Cup multiple times, including:

  • 1992
  • 1999
  • 2007
  • 2015

ICC Champions Trophy Title

South Africa won the ICC Champions Trophy once, in 1998.

ICC T20 World Cup Performances

South Africa has performed well in the ICC T20 World Cup, reaching the semi-finals on multiple occasions.

Individual Achievements

South African cricketers have achieved many individual milestones, including:

  • Most Test runs: Jacques Kallis
  • Most Test wickets: Dale Steyn
  • Highest individual Test score: Hashim Amla (311 runs)

Dominance in Limited-Overs Cricket

South Africa has shown strong performances in limited-overs cricket, with notable successes in ICC tournaments and bilateral series.
